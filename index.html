PondGuard: IoT Water Level & Temperature Monitor

PondGuard is a DIY smart monitoring solution designed to prevent pond drainage disasters and track winter thermal stratification. This project ensures 24/7 visibility into pond health, providing a safety net for aquatic life.

The Backstory: This project was born out of a near-catastrophe. A damaged pump return tube began draining my pond without any warning. I fortunately caught it just in time, but it highlighted a critical need: real-time visibility into pond health.

How It Works

The system uses a Heltec WiFi Kit 8 (ESP8266) to manage two critical safety functions:

  • Laser Level Sensing: A laser distance sensor at the top of a PVC tube measures the height of a floating ball. If the ball drops below a set threshold, an alarm is triggered.
  • Winter Thermal Profiling: Multiple DS18B20 probes monitor water temperature at different depths to ensure fish survive the winter "warm zone" at the bottom.
  • Cloud Connectivity: Real-time data is pushed to ThingSpeak for mobile monitoring and historical logging.

Hardware Specification

To ensure reliability in outdoor conditions, the electronics are housed in a transparent waterproof enclosure mounted atop the probe assembly.

  • Microcontroller: ESP8266-based Heltec Kit 8 with integrated OLED.
  • Sensors: Time-of-Flight (ToF) Laser & DS18B20 Waterproof probes.
  • Protocol: WiFi connection to ThingSpeak cloud server.
